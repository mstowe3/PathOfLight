Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga

Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this
60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture),
pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this
60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement -- you will inhale and exhale as
you flow energetically through yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.

Relax Anytime with Savasana

Prepare yourself for savasana. Lie down on your yoga mat with your arms at your side with palms
up. Close your eyes and breathe slowly but deeply. Sink into the mat and let your worries slip away.
When you are ready, roll on your side and use your arms to push yourself to a sitting position with
crossed legs. Place your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that you have in life.
Namaste.
